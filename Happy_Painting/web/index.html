  • 1

    Low Cost

    Before any product is produced, the most important consideration is how much it will consume. Once the product cost is too high, the product is likely to be eliminated. In order to avoid the problem of high product cost, I strives to produce products at the lowest cost. In the end, I managed to make a product that cost only about RM250 or less, and the function of this product is almost the same as other products.

  • 2

    Large range of painting

    In order to make the products suitable for more users, I made the size of the products larger than usual. This is because although small automatic paint spraying machines are easy to carry and have low production costs, the number of applicable people is relatively small. Small automatic painting machines are only suitable for small projects such as painting small pictures.

  • 3

    Low limitation

    The drawing method used in this product is to use a pen instead, while some other products use a laser pointer to draw. Compared with laser painting, pen painting is applicable to a larger and wider range of users. This is because the drawing material of the laser pointer can only be a hard object, such as a hard drawing board like a wooden board. For this reason, this has also led to the reduction of the application range of laser pointers.
